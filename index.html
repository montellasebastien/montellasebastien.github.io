Teamwork

“Alone we can do so little,
   together we can do so much.”

I considerably value team working. As it takes two flints to make a fire, it takes teammates to make success. I further encourage diversity and I considerably give value to volunteering. It is a great way to collaborate with outstanding people and share new insights. The experience of participating in different projects also links ambitious people together.
